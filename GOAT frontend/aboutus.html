What is G.O.A.T. ?

  • G.O.A.T. Member
  • Sep 13, 2023
  • 12 Comments

At G.O.A.T. (Greatest Of All Teams), we believe in achieving greatness not just through our products but also through the teamwork that makes them possible. We are proud to introduce ourselves as the minds behind the cutting-edge meeting booking system that is transforming the way businesses manage their schedules. Today, we want to share our journey and reveal why we are truly the G.O.A.T. when it comes to creating efficient solutions.

"The Greatest Of All Teams - G.O.A.T."

The G.O.A.T. Team: Assembling Greatness

Our journey began with eight exceptional individuals who came together to form the dream team. Four talented frontend developers and four ingenious backend developers united with a common vision - to revolutionize the way businesses conduct meetings. Our combined skills, dedication, and passion are what set us apart and make us the Greatest Of All Teams.

Why the Name G.O.A.T.?

You might be wondering, why did we choose the name G.O.A.T.? It's more than just an acronym; it embodies our commitment to excellence. In the world of sports, the term "G.O.A.T." stands for "Greatest Of All Time." We chose this name to reflect our aspiration to create the greatest meeting booking system ever.
